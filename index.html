01 / MODEL

AI maturity model

[ + ]

The five-level lens I use to diagnose where an organisation actually stands — and where the next honest step is.

Level 0
Pre-adoption
Unstructured or no AI usage. No shared practices, no visibility.
Level 1
Individual experimentation
Isolated usage without coordination. Some use AI, others don't. No link to business value.
Level 2
Isolated use cases
Teams experiment without alignment. No shared practices or guidance across the organisation.
Level 3
Coordinated adoption
Structured initiatives and shared visibility. Priorities aligned. AI initiatives tracked from idea to value.
Level 4
AI-enabled workflows
AI embedded into daily operations. People trust it, use it, and sustain it without being pushed.

I help organisations move from Levels 1–2 to Levels 3–4.

03 / CONTEXT

The context

[ + ]

Organizations are moving from digital work to AI-augmented work. This is not a tooling shift. It is a change in how work is done.

What "0 AI" really means

Many companies believe they use AI because employees use chatbots. In practice, this is still a pre-adoption stage.

  • No shared practices
  • No visibility
  • No connection to business value

AI usage remains fragmented and does not scale.

Why AI adoption fails

AI does not fail because of technology. It fails because of people, process, and data.

  • Lack of trust
  • Inconsistent usage
  • FOMO-driven initiatives
  • Data constraints
